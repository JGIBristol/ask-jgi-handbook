## Acknowledgement Guidelines

When publishing or presenting results from your ticket, please ask the client to do the following to acknowledge the support provided by the Ask‑JGI service:

- Include the following acknowledgement (replace [Student Name] with the student’s full name or “the Ask‑JGI student”):
    > "We acknowledge the support of [Student Name]  and the Jean Golding Institute, University of Bristol."
- Where appropriate, list the student as a co‑author or contributor on publications and include a short authorship/contribution statement describing the student’s role.


Also refer to [JGI acknowledgment guidelines](https://uob.sharepoint.com/sites/jean-golding/SitePages/Acknowledgement-Guidelines.aspx) for more information on acknowledging the Jean Golding Institute in general.
